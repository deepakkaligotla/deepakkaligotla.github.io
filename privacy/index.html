1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how the Budget Calculator app collects, uses, and protects your data.

2. Data We Collect

  • Financial Data: Entered expense details are stored locally on your device and never transmitted or shared.
  • Crash Logs: Collected via Firebase Crashlytics to improve app stability.
  • Analytics Data: Collected via Firebase to understand app usage and improve experience.
  • Search History: Stored only locally to show recent searches. Not shared.
  • Device Identifiers: Used for push notifications and crash analysis.

We do not collect or share personally identifiable information such as contacts, precise location, or financial account credentials.

3. How We Use Data

  • To improve app performance and diagnose crashes
  • To send optional notifications (e.g., feature updates or rating prompts)
  • To enhance the user experience, such as remembering recent searches locally

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties, except:

  • With Firebase for analytics, messaging, and diagnostics
  • If required by law or legal processes

5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Crash and analytics data may be retained by Firebase as per their policies.
  • Search history is stored only on your device and is removed if you uninstall or clear app data.

To request deletion of any associated data, contact us at your-email@example.com.

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data in transit. Firebase services comply with leading security standards.
